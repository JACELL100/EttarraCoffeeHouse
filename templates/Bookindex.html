Sustainable Coffee Workshop

Details:
Join us for an engaging and interactive Sustainable Coffee
Workshop, where we explore the journey of coffee from
farm to cup with a focus on sustainability. This workshop is
designed for coffee enthusiasts, environmental advocates,
and anyone interested in learning about how coffee
production can contribute to a greener, more
sustainable world.What to Expect:

Insightful Discussions: Learn about the environmental
impact of traditional coffee farming and the importance
of sustainable agricultural practices.

Coffee Tasting: Experience a curated selection of
sustainable coffee varieties. Discover how different
cultivation techniques affect taste and quality.

Hands-on Activities: Participate in activities that
demonstrate eco-friendly methods for coffee production,
such as composting coffee grounds and reusing coffee
waste.

Meet Experts: Interact with coffee experts, farmers,
and sustainability advocates who are leading efforts
in making the coffee industry more eco-conscious.
